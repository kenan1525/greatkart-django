DEVTÜRKS Platform Mission

DEVTÜRKS is a Government-to-Government (G2G) and Business-to-Government (B2G) cooperation platform designed to present Turkish defense and technology products under one digital roof.

The platform allows authorized institutions to express interest in products, initiate official communication processes, and coordinate international cooperation activities.

This website does not perform direct sales or commercial transactions. All interactions are conducted through official and diplomatic channels.

How It Works

Step 1: Create Your Request List

The "Add to Contract" button functions as your official presentation request list for a single product or multiple selections. It allows you to gather all necessary items to request technical details, comprehensive presentations, and other essential data in one place.

Step 2: Specify Quantities & Notes

Define the quantities for your selected product or products and include specific notes or requirements for each item in your list to ensure a tailored briefing for your organization.

Step 3: Verification & Submission

Complete your member login to authenticate your identity. At the submission stage, provide your official organization details, title, and contact information to finalize your request.

Step 4: Diplomatic Coordination & Official Briefing

Upon submission, your request is reviewed through diplomatic channels. Our coordination center will verify your inquiry and officially contact your office to arrange the necessary high-level meetings, technical presentations, and expert briefings required for your evaluation process.
