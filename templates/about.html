DEVTÜRKS Platform Mission

DEVTÜRKS is a Government-to-Government (G2G) and Business-to-Government (B2G) cooperation platform designed to present Turkish defense and technology products under one digital roof.

The platform allows authorized institutions to express interest in products, initiate official communication processes, and coordinate international cooperation activities.

This website does not perform direct sales or commercial transactions. All interactions are conducted through official and diplomatic channels.
